Key Issues and Challenges

Importance of Land-based Ecosystem

Forests,grasslands,wetlands, and other land-based habitats act as nature's lungs, absorbing carbon dioxide and releasing oxygen. These ecosysytems also mitigate climate change by sequestering carbon and influencing weather patterns

Challanges Facing Life on Land

Deforestation, habitat loss and climate change are major threads to terrestrial life. Protecting biodiversity is essential for a sustainable future.

Solution to Preserve Life on Land

Conservation efforts, sustainable practices, and reforestration projects are some of the ways to protect and restore life on land.
